Welcome to the Haven!

We are a friendly, non-swearing community aimed at providing an exiting & safe experience for everyone! Feel free to browse around our Forums, Galleries, Blogs, and our Discord & Guilded servers! Come on by, hang out & chat with us, and also join in Kenson's streams! We hope that you will enjoy your stay here with us in the Mesozoic Haven! Please, don't hesitate to Contact Us if you have any queries!

We strive to keep our community safe for people of all ages! This means that on Kensons Streams, Videos, and anything else, he does not play any rated M games, with one exception of an upcoming game (not out yet) called Deathground (Aliens but with dinosaurs basically). At most, he plays Teen rated games, as long as there is no unavoidable bad language or certain "references". There is a few exceptions to this as well, like with ARK Survival Evolved, Monster Hunter, and Path of Titans.

What is the Haven about?

In short, the Mesozoic Haven originally started under the name Jurassic Kingdoms. We originally changed our name once Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom came out since we figured it could be hard to get discovered with a big name like what Universal has done to us indirectly. But then I was told by a friend, that we might have been able to still rank for it. Oh well. We do like the name Mesozoic Haven better since it encompasses all the major dinosaur periods, instead of just one (Jurassic).

You can learn more on our About Us page.
